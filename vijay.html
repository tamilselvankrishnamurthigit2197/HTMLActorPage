Vijay Chandrasekar

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar[1][2] (born 22 June 1974[3]), known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and singer who works mainly in Tamil cinema.[4][5] He is among the highest paid actors in India[6][7] and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list on seven occasions. He has played the lead in 67 films. Referred to as Thalapathy (transl. Commander), Vijay has a significant following internationally
